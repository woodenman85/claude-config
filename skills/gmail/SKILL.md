# Gmail Skill

## Tools available
Use `mcp__*__*` Gmail tools directly — no copy-pasting needed.

## Insurance agency use cases

### Lead follow-up
- "Draft a follow-up email to a lead who filled out my life insurance form but hasn't booked yet"
- "Write a warm follow-up for someone I spoke to last week about mortgage protection"

### After appointments
- "Draft a thank-you email for my appointment today with [name] about final expense"
- "Write a recap email with next steps for a prospect I met about IUL"

### Prospecting
- "Draft a cold outreach email for mortgage protection to homeowners"
- "Write a referral request email to send to existing clients"

### Inbox management
- "Search my inbox for emails from leads this week"
- "Find all unanswered emails about insurance quotes"
- "Label all emails from GHL as 'Lead'"

## Best practices
- Always draft first — never send without showing the user first
- Keep insurance emails short, warm, and trust-building
- Include one clear next step (book a call, reply with questions, etc.)
- Never promise specific rates or coverage amounts in email
