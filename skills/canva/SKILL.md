# Canva Skill

## Tools available
Use `mcp__*__*` Canva tools directly — can create, edit, and export designs.

## Insurance agency use cases

### Social media
- "Create an Instagram post about the importance of life insurance for young families"
- "Make a Facebook post about mortgage protection — include a CTA to book a call"
- "Design a social post series (3 posts) about final expense insurance myths"

### Lead generation
- "Create a flyer for a free life insurance consultation"
- "Design a lead magnet: '5 Things to Know Before Buying Life Insurance'"
- "Make a one-page overview of our mortgage protection product"

### Client materials
- "Create a professional presentation about IUL for a client meeting"
- "Design a thank-you card to send after a policy is sold"
- "Make a referral card clients can share with friends"

### Branding
- "Create a business card design for [agent name]"
- "Design an email header for our newsletter"

## Best practices
- Check brand kit first (`list-brand-kits`) for consistent colors/fonts
- Always export and show the design before finalizing
- Keep insurance marketing compliant — no guaranteed returns, no misleading claims
- Square format for Instagram, landscape for Facebook/email headers
